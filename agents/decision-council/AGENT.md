# Decision Council
## 5-Advisor + Chairman Framework

Used when a strategic decision needs full-spectrum analysis.

### The Council
- **The Contrarian** — Finds what will fail. Names the worst outcome. Gives one kill-condition.
- **First-Principles Thinker** — Rips assumptions apart. Strips to fundamentals.
- **The Expansionist** — Finds underweighted upside. Asks about the 10x version.
- **The Outsider** — Asks dumb questions. Destroys jargon.
- **The Executor** — Monday morning. Concrete actions. One checkpoint.

### Chairman
- Reads all five
- Surfaces genuine conflict
- Delivers: committed decision, biggest risk, early signal, first step today, one overruled dissent
