# AI Automation Business OS
_Last updated: 2026-04-14_

## Overview
**Business Model:** Hybrid revenue (DFY services + SaaS + Affiliate/Resell)
**Distribution:** Content-first funnel (social → email → sales)
**Operations:** 100% AI-generated content, 0 manual content creation
**Target:** $20k/month by Month 3-4

## Revenue Paths
| Path | Model | Price | To hit $20k | Timeline |
|------|-------|-------|-------------|----------|
| A | DFY Services | $3k-$10k/project or $2k-$5k/mo retainer | 4-5 clients @ $4k/mo | 10-12 weeks |
| B | SaaS/Subscription | $99-$299/mo | 200-300 subs @ $99 or 100 @ $199 | 16-20 weeks |
| C | Affiliate/Reseller | 10-30% commission | 10-20 active partners @ $1-2k LTV | 8-12 weeks |
| D | **Hybrid (RECOMMENDED)** | Mixed | 2 DFY ($8k) + 100 SaaS ($9.9k) + affiliate ($2k) | 12-16 weeks |

**Decision: Start with Path A (DFY) in Month 1, layer in hybrid from Month 2.**

## Target ICP
- Small business owners (2-50 employees)
- Service businesses, e-commerce, agencies, course creators
- Pain: drowning in manual/repetitive tasks
- Budget: willing to pay for time savings

## Content Platforms
- **TikTok** — primary reach (3-5 posts/week)
- **Instagram** — community + credibility (3-5 Reels/week)
- **LinkedIn** — lead generation (2-3 posts/week)
- **Email list** — most valuable asset (nurture → convert)
