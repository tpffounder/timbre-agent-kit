# Revenue Roadmap — $20k/Month
_Last updated: 2026-04-14_

## The Funnel
IMPRESSIONS → (CTR 2-5%) → CLICKS → (15-25%) → LEADS → (5-10%) → QUALIFIED LEADS → (20-50%) → CUSTOMERS → REVENUE

## Platform KPI Targets

### TikTok
- Posts/week: 3-5
- Avg impressions/post: 300-1k
- Engagement rate: 3-8%
- Monthly impressions: 10k-20k
- CTR to link: 1-2%

### Instagram
- Posts/week: 3-5 Reels
- Engagement rate: 2-5%
- Monthly impressions: 5k-10k
- Link clicks: 50-200/mo

### LinkedIn
- Posts/week: 2-3
- Avg impressions/post: 500-2k
- Engagement rate: 2-4%
- DM inquiries/month: 10-20
- DM → call conversion: 40-60%

### Email List
- Month 1: 100-200 subscribers
- Month 2: 300-500 subscribers
- Month 3: 800-1,200 subscribers
- Open rate: 25-40%
- Click rate: 3-8%
- List-to-customer: 5-10%

## Week-by-Week Plan

### Week 1: Foundation
- Define ICP
- Create positioning statement
- Set up 3 landing pages (audit, service, tripwire)
- Configure email platform + write 5-email nurture sequence
- Generate first week of content
- Schedule posts to Buffer
- Set up analytics tracking
- Prep 20-person warm outreach list

### Week 2: Launch
- Go live across all platforms
- Begin warm outreach (20 DMs)
- Monitor engagement daily
- Adjust content based on early data

### Week 3-4: Optimize
- Double down on what's working
- Begin cold outreach
- First sales calls
- Close first DFY client

### Month 2: Scale
- Add SaaS/affiliate stream
- Grow email list aggressively
- Systemize client delivery
