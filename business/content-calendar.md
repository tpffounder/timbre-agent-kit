- **Goal:** Stop scroll, create recognition

### Middle of Funnel (Consideration)  
- **Long-form posts:** Detailed solutions, case studies, frameworks

### Bottom of Funnel (Conversion)
- **Email sequences:** Personalized follow-up, specific offers
- **DM conversations:** Qualification, consultation booking
- **Goal:** Schedule calls, close deals

## Cross-Platform Strategy

### TikTok
- **Audience:** Younger business owners, early adopters
- **Content:** Fast-paced, trend-aligned, hook-heavy
- **CTA:** DM-driven for immediate engagement

### Instagram Reels
- **Audience:** Established business owners, 30-50
- **Content:** Slightly more polished, square format
- **CTA:** Link in bio for consultation booking

### YouTube Shorts
- **Audience:** Problem-solvers, DIY business owners
- **Content:** Tutorial-style, educational
- **CTA:** Subscribe + watch longer content

### LinkedIn
- **Audience:** B2B decision makers, service business owners
- **Content:** Professional, case-study driven
- **CTA:** Comment engagement, profile visits

### Email
- **Audience:** Warm leads, existing contacts
- **Content:** Deep dives, exclusive insights
- **CTA:** Reply directly, schedule calls
