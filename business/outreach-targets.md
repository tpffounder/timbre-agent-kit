## Target Industries (Priority Order)

### 1. Cleaning Services
**Why:** High manual scheduling, high turnover, time-intensive
**Sub-categories:**
- Residential cleaning
- Commercial cleaning
- Carpet cleaning
- Window cleaning
- Janitorial services

**Pain points:**
- Scheduling chaos (last-minute changes)
- Employee turnover (75%+ annual)
- Invoice/payment tracking
- Customer communication overload

**Automation opportunities:**
- Automated scheduling + reminders
- Employee onboarding/training workflows
- Invoice automation + payment tracking
- Customer feedback/review requests

### 2. Landscaping & Lawn Care
**Why:** Seasonal, weather-dependent, route optimization
**Sub-categories:**
- Residential lawn care
- Commercial landscaping
- Tree services
- Irrigation/sprinkler services

**Pain points:**
- Route optimization (fuel/time waste)
- Weather cancellations/rescheduling
- Equipment maintenance tracking
- Seasonal employee hiring/training

**Automation opportunities:**
- Dynamic scheduling based on weather
- Route optimization algorithms
- Equipment maintenance reminders
- Seasonal hiring workflows

### 3. HVAC & Plumbing
**Why:** Emergency calls, parts inventory, technician dispatch
**Sub-categories:**
- Residential HVAC
- Commercial HVAC
- Plumbing services
- Electrical services

**Pain points:**
- Emergency call triage
- Technician availability/scheduling
- Parts inventory management
- Follow-up/maintenance scheduling

**Automation opportunities:**
- Intelligent call routing
- Technician dispatch optimization
- Inventory reorder alerts
- Preventive maintenance scheduling

### 4. Home Services
**Why:** Project-based, client communication intensive
**Sub-categories:**
- Painting contractors
- Roofing companies

- Remodeling contractors

**Pain points:**
- Project timeline tracking
- Material ordering/delivery
- Client communication overload

**Automation opportunities:**

- Payment reminder systems

### 5. Professional Services
**Why:** Admin-heavy, client management intensive
**Sub-categories:**
- Marketing agencies
- Accounting firms

**Pain points:**
- Time tracking/billing

**Automation opportunities:**

## Company Size Targeting

### Small (2-10 employees)
**Characteristics:**
- Owner does everything
- Limited budget but high pain
- Quick decision making
- Willing to try new solutions

**Approach:**
- Focus on time savings for owner
- Simple, quick-to-implement solutions
- Monthly pricing <$1,000
- "Get your weekends back" messaging

### Medium (11-50 employees)  
**Characteristics:**
- Some team structure
- Growing pains evident
- Budget for solutions
- Longer sales cycle

**Approach:**
- Focus on scalability/growth
- Team efficiency improvements
- ROI-based justification
- "Scale without adding overhead" messaging

## Qualification Criteria (ICP Score 1-10)

### Company Fit (0-4 points)
- **4:** Exact industry match + right size + manual processes evident
- **3:** Industry match + right size
- **2:** Industry match but wrong size OR wrong industry but right characteristics
- **1:** Marginal fit
- **0:** Not a fit

### Pain Point Evidence (0-3 points)
- **3:** Clear evidence of manual processes (social media posts, reviews, website content)
- **2:** Likely pain points based on industry norms
- **1:** Possible pain points
- **0:** No evidence

### Growth Signals (0-2 points)
- **2:** Hiring, expanding, recent funding/news
- **1:** Steady business, active social media
- **0:** Stagnant/low activity

### Contact Accessibility (0-1 point)
- **1:** Direct email/LinkedIn available
- **0:** Hard to contact

**Scoring:**
- **8-10:** High priority (contact within 24 hours)
- **6-7:** Medium priority (contact within 72 hours)
- **4-5:** Low priority (batch outreach)
- **0-3:** Don't contact (not ICP)

## Outreach Strategy

### Week 1-2: Testing & Learning
- **Target:** 20 companies/week
- **Industries:** 2-3 different ones
- **Approach:** Test different messaging angles
- **Goal:** Identify which industries/messaging works best

### Week 3-4: Scaling
- **Target:** 50-65 companies/week
- **Industries:** Focus on 1-2 best performers
- **Approach:** Refined messaging based on learnings
- **Goal:** 1 closed client by end of Week 4

### Outreach Mix:
- **LinkedIn:** 60% (warm, professional)
- **Email:** 30% (follow-up, detailed)
- **Social Media DMs:** 10% (engagement-based)

### Messaging Angles to Test:
1. **Time savings:** "Get 10+ hours back per week"
2. **Growth enablement:** "Scale without adding overhead"
3. **Pain relief:** "Stop the scheduling chaos"
4. **Employee retention:** "Reduce 75% turnover"
5. **Customer satisfaction:** "Improve customer experience"
