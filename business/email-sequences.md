## Sequence 1: Warm Lead Follow-up
**Trigger:** DM reply, form submission, content engagement
**Goal:** Schedule consultation call
**Length:** 3 emails over 7 days

### Email 1: Immediate (Day 0)
**Subject:** Re: [Their query from DM/form]
**Timing:** Within 1 hour

```
Hi [First Name],

Thanks for reaching out about [specific pain point they mentioned].

I help service business owners like you automate the manual work that eats up their week — things like [mention 1-2 relevant pain points based on their query].

The result is usually 10+ hours back per week and fewer "11pm Tuesday" moments trying to catch up.

Would you be open to a quick 20-minute call next week to see if we could build something similar for your business?

Best,
Joan

P.S. Here's a 60-second example of what we built for a [their industry] client: [link to relevant case study/short-form video]
```

### Email 2: Value Add (Day 3)
**Subject:** A quick idea for [Their Business Name]
**Timing:** 3 days later if no reply

```
Hi [First Name],

Following up on my previous email — I was reviewing some [their industry] businesses this week and noticed a common pattern:

Most owners spend [X hours] per week on [specific manual task] when it could be fully automated for about [time/cost estimate].

The system we'd build would:
1. [Benefit 1 specific to their business]
2. [Benefit 2 specific to their business]  
3. [Benefit 3 specific to their business]

Still open to a quick chat next week?

Best,
Joan
```

### Email 3: Breakup (Day 7)
**Subject:** Closing this thread
**Timing:** 7 days after first email

```
Hi [First Name],

I'll close this thread for now since I haven't heard back.

If you ever want to explore automating [their specific pain point] in the future, just reply to this email.

In the meantime, here's a free resource that might help: [link to relevant blog post/guide]

All the best,
Joan
```

## Best Practices

### Timing
- **Warm leads:** Respond within 1 hour
- **Cold outreach:** Tuesday-Thursday, 10AM-2PM local time
- **Follow-ups:** 3-4 day intervals
- **Breakup emails:** 7-14 days after last contact

### Personalization
- Always use first name
- Reference specific pain points mentioned
- Mention their company/industry
- Link to relevant content/case studies

### Testing
- A/B test subject lines
- Track open rates, reply rates
- Adjust sequences based on performance
- Update templates quarterly

### Integration
- Connect to CRM for tracking
- Automate with n8n workflows
- Sync with Notion pipeline
- Report performance to Agent 6
